What is Tryb?

Tryb helps people form real communities through shared activities — not profiles. It’s built around the idea that belonging comes from participation, not swiping or scrolling.

Every connection on Tryb starts with something to do — from games and creative workshops to casual hangouts. Short, low-pressure activities evolve into lasting communities with rituals and identity.

Meet. Play. Belong. That’s the Tryb loop — discover people through activities, participate together, and stay connected through tribes that feel real.

At the intersection of social, gaming, and discovery, Tryb reimagines digital connection for a generation that values experiences over endless feeds.

Meet the Founder

Akhil KT, Founder & CEO of Tryb, is a dual-degree graduate from IIT Madras with nearly a decade of experience building and scaling digital products across gaming, fintech, and B2B commerce.

Before founding Tryb, he led product strategy and growth at Gameberry Labs (creators of Ludo Star and Jackaroo), Bzaar.com, and Thrive, helping products reach millions of users globally.

Akhil’s mission with Tryb is simple — to make online interactions feel genuine again, built around play, discovery, and belonging.
